About Us

Transforming Education Since 2005

EduSuccess Institute was founded with a vision to provide quality education to students preparing for competitive exams and school curriculum. What started as a small coaching center has now grown into one of the most trusted names in education.

Our mission is to nurture young minds, instill confidence, and provide the right guidance to help students achieve their academic goals. We believe in holistic development that goes beyond just exam preparation.

Over the years, we have helped thousands of students secure admissions in top engineering and medical colleges, as well as excel in their school examinations.

Documents Required for Admission

Recent passport size photographs (2 copies)
School ID card or bonafide certificate
Previous year's mark sheet (photocopy)
Aadhaar card (photocopy)
Address proof (if different from Aadhaar)
